STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Title: Data Backup and Recovery
SOP Number: IT-001
Department: Information Technology
Version: 1.2
Effective Date: 2026-01-15

1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this document is to outline the standard procedures for performing data backups and recovery operations to ensure data integrity and business continuity.

2. SCOPE
This procedure applies to all IT personnel responsible for managing critical company servers and databases.

3. RESPONSIBILITIES
IT Administrators are required to ensure daily backups are completed and verified.

4. PROCEDURE
4.1 Daily Backups
The system shall automatically run incremental backups every night at 02:00 AM.
Personnel must use the designated backup tool.
Do not store backups on the local drive.

4.2 Weekly Full Backups
Full backups must be executed every Sunday at 01:00 AM.
